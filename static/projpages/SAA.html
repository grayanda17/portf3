San Diego Comic Con "Super Asian America" and Mockups

Project Details


My Role:

Graphic Designer

Summary:

I was commissioned to create video and marketing material for Racebending.com's San Diego Comic Con 2017 panel "Super Asian America". This included creating video slides that introduced this year's panelists of Asian-American directors, actors, and writers. Tributes to other organizations include UC San Diego's Vietnamese Student Association and the San Diego Asian Film Festival. The former had opened up a T-shirt design contest, which I entered with a shirt design featured an abstract Vietnamese flag with a year of the monkey festival theme.
